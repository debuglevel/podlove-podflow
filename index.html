Podflow

[experimental] a plugin for podlove to automate the workflow of processing and publishing a podcast episode

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

What is Podflow?

Podflow is a @Podlove plugin to make it as easy as possible to publish new Podcast episodes.

How does it work?

It just brings together two really great tools: Podlove and Auphonic. Have a look on this short video to see Podflow in action: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mSxsbvxdRpE (unfortunately only available in german)

Development

I actually do not know, if I will continue developing the plugin. I guess the podlove team wants develop its own podflow-alike tool in the future. Therefore, podflow is rather a temporary solution, and I'm looking forward to the development of podlove which will probably create an way better solution than Podflow is at the moment.
